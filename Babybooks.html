In Our Community


SKT Academy supports and participates in community events. Some of these activities include supporting the local YMCA, PTA, Quality Care for Children, and various sponsored events. Our support further extends in the community by supporting local clubs and associations (softball, baseball, scouts, etc.).


SKT partners with colleges and universities by sponsoring student internships and inviting students to observe in the classrooms.


SKT participates in programs aimed to spread the word about quality childcare and the impact of early childhood education.


We enjoy visiting local HOA Community Centers for Story Time or Special Favorite Activities. Finally, we believe in promoting our parent’s businesses through our Parent Business Directory.



Making a Difference… Charities & Fundraisers


SKT believes that it is both a calling and privilege to serve others. Whether it is serving in the community or being apart of a larger humanitarian effort, there are many opportunities to make a positive difference in the lives of others. At Favorite Time everyone is encouraged to participate and help with efforts that encourage caring, giving, and helping others.
